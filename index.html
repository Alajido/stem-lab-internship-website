Stem Lab Interns Site

Stemlab is creating a generation of ecosystem that enhances tech entrepreneurship in young innovators to help launch, scale and amplify their impact to foster development and growth. We have development programs that equip, inspire, innovation and empower tech entrepreneurship in young Africans.

Missions

To create an enacting environment for young innovations and startups with the right education, network, tools, skills and market required for them to build, deploy sustainable and profitable solutions, products, processes and systems to grow the Nigerian economy.
