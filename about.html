About

We are an ecosystem built to support ICT focused tech entrepreneurs and young individuals integrating the STEM pedagogy in Procom. We have built a framework for innovative minds create great solutions, enterprises and companies to solve most of the biggest challenge in the society. We created a company focused in creating an enabling environment for most startups. STEMLAB Arewa was established to empower, promote and position Africa especially Nigeria in technology and startup ecosystem with a vision of using technology as a strategy to combat unemployment.
